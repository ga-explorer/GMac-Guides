Have You Ever Been ...

- Amazed by the geometric patterns all around you in natural and human designs
- Eager to study geometry but found its mathematics difficult and obscure
- Searching for a symbolic language to fluently communicate geometric ideas
- Struggling to design software for computing with geometry to realize our own geometric ideas and patterns

Meet Geometric Algebra ...

- A universal algebraic language for expressing geometric objects and transformations far superior to vector and matrix algebras
- A fascinating mathematical structure with geometrically significant, dimension independent, and coordinates-independent expressions
- A solid and complete mathematical base for developing software for geometric computing applications in all fields of science and enginering

And With GMac You Can ...

- Express your geometric ideas with a simple Domain Specific Language (DSL) based directly on Geometric Algebra
- Symbolically and visually explore the DSL code through a powerful scripting interface, with Wolfram Mathematica in the background, to gain deeper understanding of your geometric ideas
- Generate efficient implementations of your DSL code in any programming language, from simple subroutines to complete computational libraries with any file structure you desire
- Study how GMac itself is built as an open source compiler so you can use the same ideas for your own projects
